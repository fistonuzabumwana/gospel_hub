# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 2026**

Gospel Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Google Account Information
When you opt to use the Cloud Backup & Restore feature and log in via Google Sign-In, we collect:
*   **Your Name & Email Address**: Used to identify your account and personalize the sync interface.
*   **Profile Picture URL**: Used solely to display your avatar inside the App's settings header.

### B. Application Data
To enable the backup and sync features, we back up the following data to your personal cloud:
*   **Preferences**: Your selected reading language, bible translation preferences, and theme settings.
*   **Reading History**: Your recently read Bible books and chapters.
*   **User Creations**: Your custom notes, bookmarks/favorites, tag lists, verse highlights, and hymn playlists.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the App's features, specifically:
*   To back up your notes, highlights, and playlists so you can restore them if you switch devices.
*   To authorize and write backup files directly to your personal Google Drive **Application Data Folder** (`appDataFolder`). 

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We interact only with the following official third-party services:
*   **Google Sign-In**: Used for user authentication.
*   **Google Drive API**: Used to upload and retrieve your backup files.

Please review the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how Google handles your data.

---

## 4. Data Security and Retention

*   **Local Storage**: Your reading history, highlights, notes, and playlists are stored locally on your device in a secure SQLite database.
*   **Cloud Backups**: Backups are uploaded directly from your device to Google Drive's secure servers. We do not host or store your personal data on any external servers owned by us.
*   **Retention**: Your data is stored as long as you choose to keep the App installed or keep your backup file on Google Drive.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

*   **Sign Out**: You can sign out of Google Sign-In at any time from the settings screen to disable synchronization.
*   **Delete Backup Data**: You can delete your cloud backup file at any time by revoking Gospel Hub's access to your Google Account via the Google Account Permissions Page.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or updating the "Last Updated" date.
